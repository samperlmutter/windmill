WINDMILL
By Team Green Shorts
Chris Mineweaser, Kristin Pflug, Sam Perlmutter, and Don Robinson

INSTRUCTIONS
-Use the arrow keys to move around
-Search the desert area for parts; walk over them to pick them up
-Once you have all 4 parts, return to the windmill and press F to fix the windmill
-Press R to restart the game

CHEAT CODES
Press W to instantly collect all 4 parts
Press T to set the timer to 5
Press K to reset the game room
